(Wh)Y Tejas?

I say, why not?



Hello! I am Tejas, 29, a resident of Paris, France.

In my early teens, I created a Harry Potter fan-site using HTML-based software. Back then, I also chose my first Gmail ID using the initial of my last name (Y) and my first name (Tejas). Over the years, people have misconstrued it as a pun on "Why Tejas?". Paradoxically, I consider myself a rational-creative who continually asks "Why not?" of himself and of life.

I am teaching myself web-development from scratch. So, everything you see on here has been created by me. I plan to add more content and styles as I continue learning. For now, feel free to browse my book reviews.
