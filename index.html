(Wh)y Tejas?

I say, why not?

Hello! I am Tejas. I am 29 years old and I live in Paris, France. I love literature, science, poetry and philosophy. I am also passionate about learning new languages, finding the best coffee shops, traveling and photography.

In my early teens, I created a 'Harry Potter' fan-site using HTML-based software. Back then, I chose my first gmail ID using the initial of my last name (Y) and my first name (Tejas). Over the years, people have misconstrued it as a pun on "Why Tejas?".
Ironically, I consider myself a rational-creative who continually asks "Why not?" of himself and of life.

I am teaching myself web-development from scratch. So, everything you see on here has been created by me. I plan to add more content and styles as I continue learning. For now, feel free to browse my latest book and movie reviews.

Thanks for stopping by today!
